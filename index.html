What Does Bethville Poultry Farm Do?

Bethville Poultry Farm's Purpose:
This website will be able to cover all aspects of chicken farming. This includes incubation and egg selection, proper feeding formulation, medication and veterinary services, housing and cleaning management and various types of chicken breeds in the market.This website will offer insights on various ways of marketing poultry products. I strongly believe that this website will help improve both the farmers' and consumers life. Isn't that a great thing!

Who Does Bethville Poultry Farm Target?

Bethville's Poultry Farm audience:
Well, this website mainly targets poultry farmers from all around the country. There has been a great challenge with poultry farmers accessing ready market for their produce. This is just the right website for that! Also, the consumers can as be enlightened on the best breeds in the market. However, this website just mainly focuses on the poultry farmers. They will receive insights from poultry experts as well.
