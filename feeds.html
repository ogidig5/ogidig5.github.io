Elements or ingredients required for feeding include: carbohydrates, proteins, vitamins, minerals and non-nutrients. Carbohydrates should be about 75%, proteins 15%, vitamins 5%, minerals 3% and non-nutrients 2%. Examples of carbohydrates include pollard (32/= per kg), maize bran (20/= per kg) and maize jam (28/= per kg).
